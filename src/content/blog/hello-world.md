Welcome to the official Hummingbird blog! We're excited to share our journey in building the next generation of desktop music players.

## Why Hummingbird?

Hummingbird was born out of a desire for a music player that is both powerful and efficient. Many modern players are bloated and slow; we wanted something that feels instant.

### Key Features

- **Blazing Fast**: Written with performance in mind.
- **Privacy First**: Your data stays on your machine.
- **Customizable**: Themes, layouts, and more.

Stay tuned for more updates as we approach our 1.0 release!
